# User Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: February 15, 2026

If you want your data deleted from AutoDM-Test, follow the steps below.

## 1. What Data We May Hold

Depending on usage, we may hold:

- webhook event records (comment IDs, text, usernames, metadata);
- processing logs and error logs;
- limited operational data required for reliability and security.

## 2. How to Request Deletion

Send a deletion request to: `coupleofpai@gmail.com`

Include:

- your Instagram username;
- the Instagram account linked to the request;
- approximate date range of activity;
- any identifiers that help locate records (optional).

## 3. Verification

To protect account security, we may request reasonable verification that you control or are authorized for the relevant account.

## 4. Deletion Timeline

After successful verification:

- we acknowledge the request;
- we delete eligible data from active systems;
- we process deletion from backups on the next scheduled retention cycle where technically feasible.

## 5. Exceptions

We may retain limited data when required by law, security obligations, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution.

## 6. Meta Platform Data

Data held by Meta/Instagram must be managed through their own platform controls and policies.

## 7. Contact

For deletion requests, contact: `coupleofpai@gmail.com`
